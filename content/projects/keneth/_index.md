KENETh (Kotlin Energy NETwork Host) is a simulation framework for modeling and analyzing energy distribution networks.

## Overview

KENETh provides tools for:
- Modeling energy network topologies
- Simulating power flow and distribution
- Analyzing network behavior under various conditions
